morning haven _

on a good morning, practice a meditative yoga flow.


rise with the sun - surya namaskar (sun salutation)

swaying with the trees - vrikshasana (tree)

firm as a mountain - tadasana (mountain)

bold, - virabhadrasana i (warrior 1)

strong, - virabhadrasana ii (warrior 2)

humble - baddha virabhadrasana (humble warrior)

finding our way - utthita parshvakonasana (side angle)

existing - virabhadrasana i (warrior 1)

being one with the creatures - bidalasana (cat cow)

remembering where we come from - bālāsana (child)

imagining where we'll go - bhujangasana (cobra)

thanking oneself - siddhasana (sitting)

.. - shavasana (corpse)
